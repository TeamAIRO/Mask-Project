While reopening the society in a post-pandemic era, it is extremely important to follow adequate safety measures to avoid another Covid19 outbreak. Safety starts with prevention, good individual behavior as well as monitoring of population risk in high density areas such as schools or offices.

With this in mind, we decided to create SamurAI - an AI powered multi-tiered safety monitoring system that can

  1. Prevent risk by enforcing safety measures such as use of mask or temparature regulation. 
  2. Detect an individual with or without mask and monitor individual adherence and risk profile towards safety.
  3. Monitor population of people in high density areas to monitor adherence of group of people and determine risk profile.

We designed 3 AI powered algorithms to achieve the above goals:

  1. Algorithm that detects whether someone is wearing a mask or not. This can be easily used in public places to prevent entry of people who do not wear mask or have temparature
  2. Algorithm that identifies the person with or without mask based on facial recognition.  This can be used to create an individual profile for safety adherence for that individual and intervention if necessary.
  3. Algorith that detects potentially dangerous movements such as touching face (ear, eyes, mouth) which increases individual risk as well as risk of spread to others

We believe these algorithms can be used together to achieve the goals of SamurAI and make the post-pandemic world a much safer place.  Lets "Outsmart the virus with AI".
